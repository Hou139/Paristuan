    Local insights

    Before you wander

    Practical advice gathered from locals to keep your Paris adventure seamless.

    Reserve ahead

    Boutique restaurants book quickly. Reserve dinner spots two weeks out, especially for weekend evenings.

    Embrace the tempo

    Many shops pause midday. Plan cultural visits for mornings and evenings, leaving afternoons for leisurely cafés.

    Travel light

    Parisian streets often include cobblestones and staircases. Opt for a compact suitcase and comfortable footwear.

    Mind the etiquette

    Start every conversation with a warm “Bonjour” and keep voices at a gentle tone in intimate venues.
